Welcome to Coro-Nah!

With more and more places starting to reopen after lockdown, it can be difficult knowing which restaurants, theaters, gyms, etc. are following proper guidelines and are safe to visit.

Coro-Nah solves this problem, allowing you to view ratings assessing the safety of any public places, and also rate the safety of any places you have been to, helping mitigate the further spread of COVID-19.
